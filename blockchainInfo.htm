General Info about Blockchain

What are cryptocurrencies?
Cryptocurrencies are digital assets living on a blockchain that can be exchanged for other digital assets or for fiat money, such as the euro or the US dollar.

What is a blockchain?
Blockchain is a technology based on the science of cryptography allowing secure recording of digital transactions and the creation of unique digital rights.

What is USDC?
USDC is a "stablecoin," a cryptocurrency with a value always equal to 1 US Dollar (USD).
https://coinmarketcap.com/de/currencies/usd-coin/

How does USDC maintain its value (peg) of 1 USD?
USDC is a fully colletaralized fiat backed stablecoin. That is, for every USDC, there is a US Dollar stored in a bank. In addition, market incentives are in place to ensure the stability of its peg.

What do I need to receive USDC?
To receive the payment in USDC you need a cryptocurrency wallet for the Avalanche blockchain. If you don't have one right now, you can create it after the experiment.

Avalanche Blockchain
The Avalanche Blockchain is a new and ambitious blockchain that supports smart contracts and is Ethereum compatible. Avalanche uses a novel Proof of Stake mechanism with high scalability, resulting in low transaction fees and faster transactions.
